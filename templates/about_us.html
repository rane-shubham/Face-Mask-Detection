ABOUT

  • Face Mask Detection System

    A face mask detection is a system capable of detecting if one or more individuals is/are wearing their face mask. With the increasing number of COVID-19 victims, integrating systems capable of detecting people wearing a face mask properly or not will help us track them easily.
